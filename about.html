I examine the evolution of planetary interiors through the lens of mantle geochemistry and igneous processes. I integrate melt inclusion analysis, stable isotope geochemistry, experimental petrology, and thermodynamic modelling to resolve how volatiles and trace elements are stored, mobilised, and concentrated during mantle melting. My work identifies the links between mantle source heterogeneity, magma chemistry, and surface mineral systems, with applications spanning Earth's lithosphere to the Martian mantle.
