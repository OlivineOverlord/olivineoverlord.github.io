Mantle Carbon Cycling

Using carbon isotopes in olivine-hosted melt inclusions to trace volatile pathways from the lithospheric mantle to eruptive melts. Applications span alkaline magmatism and metasomatism.

Martian Magmatism

Experimental and geochemical constraints on the mineralogy and melt generation of Mars’ interior. This work informs planetary evolution and sample return mission strategies.
