Current Research Projects

🌋 Mantle Carbon

Investigating carbon storage and mobilization in Earth's deep mantle through high-precision isotope analysis and experimental petrology. Understanding how CO₂ moves from mantle to surface.

Read more →

🇦🇺 Eastern Australian Volcanism

Exploring the geochemical evolution of intraplate volcanic provinces in eastern Australia, focusing on volatile-rich alkaline magmas and their mantle sources.

Read more →

🔴 Martian Mantle Melting

Constraining the composition and melting behavior of Mars' interior through experimental studies and meteorite analysis to support future sample return missions.

Read more →

🔬 Analytical Techniques

Developing new SIMS protocols for carbon isotopes in silicate glasses and advancing melt inclusion analysis through correlation matrix approaches.

Read more →

Across these projects, I'm exploring how volatiles and trace components shape magmas, from Earth's mantle to Mars. My work combines experimental petrology, geochemical analysis, and planetary thinking. I care about fundamental science, long-term insight, and publishing in a way that invites others in.
